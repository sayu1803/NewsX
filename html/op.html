OnePlus 12 launch date officially revealed, check out confirmed specs

Written By XYZ April 14, 2021

Technology

OnePlus 12 is an upcoming mobile by OnePlus. The phone is rumoured to come with a 120 Hz refresh rate 6.70-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 1440x3216 pixels (QHD+) at a pixel density of 525 pixels per inch (ppi). The display sports Gorilla Glass for protection.OnePlus 12 come with 16GB of RAM. The OnePlus 12 is rumoured to run Android 14 and is expected to be powered by a 5400mAh non-removable battery. The OnePlus 12 supports Super VOOC fast charging. As far as the cameras are concerned, the OnePlus 12 is rumoured to pack a triple rear camera setup featuring a 64-megapixel primary camera; a 50-megapixel camera, and a 50-megapixel camera. It is rumoured to sport a single front camera setup for selfies, a 32-megapixel primary camera. The OnePlus 12 runs OxygenOS 14 is based on Android 14 and packs 256GB of inbuilt storage. The OnePlus 12 is tipped to be a dual-SIM (GSM and GSM) mobile that will accept Nano-SIM and Nano-SIM cards. It is said to launch in Eternal Green and Titan Black colours. It features an IP68 rating for dust and water protection. Connectivity options on the OnePlus 12 are said to include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax, GPS, NFC, 3G, 4G (with support for Band 40 used by some LTE networks in India) , and 5G with active 4G on both SIM cards. Sensors on the phone are rumoured to include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, compass/ magnetometer, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and in-display fingerprint sensor. The OnePlus 12 supports face unlock.

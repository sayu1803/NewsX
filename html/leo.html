Thalapathy Vijay's 'Leo' all set to stream on Netflix on this date

Written By XYZ April 14, 2021

Entertainment

Thalapathy Vijay and director Lokesh Kanagaraj's 'Leo' is one of the biggest hits from Tamil cinema this year. Reportedly, the film is said to have earned over Rs 600 crore worldwide. Now, 'Leo' is all set for its OTT release after a successful month in theatres. The OTT platform announced the film's streaming date on their social media pages. It is said that the film was sold for a record price to Netflix. THALAPATHY VIJAY'S 'LEO' TO STREAM ON NETFLIX ON THIS DATE Directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, 'Leo' is an action thriller, which released in theatres on October 19. The film managed to earn over Rs 600 crore worldwide. On November 20, the official X (formerly Twitter) handle of Netflix India South announced the streaming date of 'Leo'. They wrote, "The wait is finally over!! We have some Bloody Sweet news for you. Naa Ready!
